Our Story

Three Generations of Fabric Mastery.

From a small workshop in Gandhi Nagar in 1938 to one of Delhi's most trusted fabric manufacturers. 87 years of weaving tradition, innovation, and excellence.

Heritage

Our Journey Through the Decades.

1938

Founded in Gandhi Nagar, Delhi. Started as a small silk trading workshop serving local tailors and designers.

1960s

Second generation takes charge. Expanded into brocade and jacquard weaving. Established supply relationships with Varanasi artisans.

1990s

Pioneered embroidery integration — Kasab Zari, Badla, and sequence work. Grew to serve 200+ wholesale accounts across India.

2010s

Third generation leadership. Modernised production with digital printing and CAD design. Obtained IEC registration for international exports.

Today

5,000+ designs across 15 categories. Serving fashion brands, export houses, and individual buyers worldwide through our D2C and wholesale channels.

Our Philosophy

Every Thread Tells a Story.
